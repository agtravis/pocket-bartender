Welcome!

Here's how it works...

Start adding stuff to your liquor cabinet!

  • Type in the name of an ingredient you like in the search bar
  • When prompted, select 'Add to My Cabinet
  • The ingredient should appear!
  • Your cabinet never gets full, you can scroll to see any hidden ingredients.
  • To remove an item, click on it and select '86'd'

Whether or not an ingredient is in your cabinet, you can see some cool drinks ideas by selecting 'Display Recipes' instead.

If you are counting calories, don't worry, we have you covered! Estimated calorie counts are calculated when you choose to see the recipe for a drink that looks good. Choose to see the recipe by clicking on the 'Make This Drink Button'!
